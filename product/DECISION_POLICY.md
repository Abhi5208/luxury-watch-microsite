# Decision Policy

## Purpose

Define how project decisions are made.

## Rules

- Decisions must have an owner.
- Decisions must record rationale.
- Scope-impacting decisions require approval.
- Unresolved decisions must be tracked in `OPEN_DECISIONS.md`.
