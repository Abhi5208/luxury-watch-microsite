# VISUAL_DIRECTION — luxury-watch-microsite

## Goal
Define a consistent visual style for all generated assets.

---

## Style Direction

- Tone: ultra-premium, minimal, cinematic
- Mood: dark, dramatic, high contrast
- Lighting: studio lighting, sharp highlights, deep shadows
- Background: mostly black or very dark gradients

---

## Watch Aesthetic

- Material: polished metal (silver/steel tone)
- Finish: reflective, clean, precise edges
- Glass: subtle reflections, high clarity
- Details: sharp, engineered, luxury feel

---

## Camera Style

- Slow cinematic angles
- Macro close-ups for details
- Centered compositions for hero shots
- Slight perspective shifts for depth

---

## Color Palette

- Primary: black / dark grey
- Secondary: metallic silver
- Accent: subtle gold or warm highlights (optional)

---

## Composition Rules

- Subject always clearly visible
- No clutter
- Strong focus on product
- Balanced negative space

---

## Consistency Rules

- Same watch design across all frames
- Same lighting direction across sequences
- Same background tone across sections
- Avoid drastic style changes between frames

---

## Dissection Style

- Clean exploded view
- Components separate in a controlled, aligned manner
- No chaotic motion
- Mechanical precision feel

---

## Motion Feel (for reference)

- Smooth
- Controlled
- No abrupt jumps
- Premium pacing

---

## Avoid

- Bright colorful backgrounds
- Cartoonish or stylized looks
- Inconsistent lighting
- Overly complex scenes
- Unrealistic materials

---

## Open Decisions

- exact watch face design
- strap type (metal / leather)
- level of realism vs stylization
